# 빈방의 메아리를 임대하는 도시 에코렌트

장르: Urban Fantasy / Mystery Simulation

When a person leaves a room in Echorent, they do not only leave dust, heat, and fingerprints. They leave a thin acoustic residue called an Echoprint: a layered trace of habits, emotional spikes, and unfinished intentions. Most cities treat these residues as urban noise. Echorent built an economy on them.

The city’s oldest district, Hollow Ward, is mapped not by streets but by resonance zones. A former violin teacher’s apartment still hums with disciplined rhythm. A bankrupt trader’s office carries split-second risk calculations. A child’s attic room amplifies fearless improvisation. Through licensed Echo Chambers, citizens can temporarily lease these Echoprints and overlay them onto their own cognition.

This made Echorent the fastest-learning city in the world. Apprentices train in days instead of years. Negotiators borrow calm from monks’ cells. Surgeons rehearse with the steady pulse of long-dead masters. But the borrowed pattern is never neutral. Echoprints remember what their owners could not resolve, and those unresolved fragments leak.

Recently, the city has entered a civic crisis called Resonance Drift. Residents report finishing strangers’ sentences, waking with vivid memories of lives they never lived, and developing compulsions tied to buildings they have never visited. If too many citizens lease from the same high-intensity Echoprint, identity overlap events occur: synchronized speech, mirrored gestures, even collective panic.

The Council refuses to shut down leasing, because Echorent’s prosperity depends on it. Instead, players enter as Registry Weavers, specialists who audit dangerous Echoprints, trace contamination chains, and decide which memories can be circulated as public utility and which must be sealed forever.

## 주요 시스템 (Mechanics)
1. **Echo Leasing Slots:** Characters equip limited Echoprints that grant temporary skill boosts, behavior modifiers, and dialogue options.
2. **Resonance Stability Meter:** Overuse of high-intensity Echoprints raises Drift risk, causing identity bleed events and altered mission outcomes.
3. **Memory Quarantine Protocols:** Players can seal, dilute, or redistribute Echoprints, changing district economies and faction trust.
4. **Building-Attuned Investigation:** Clues are hidden in acoustic layers of rooms; scanning at different frequencies reveals different truth states.
5. **Civic Identity Index:** City-wide policy choices determine whether Echorent becomes a cooperative memory commons, a controlled surveillance regime, or collapses into echo-anarchy.
